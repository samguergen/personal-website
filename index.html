Sam Guergenenov
I'm a web developer living in New York City and originally from Paris, France. While studying Philosophy and Economics at Wellesley College, I helped with the publication of a statistical research paper and started working as a technical support for a small IT company. I realized that my true passion was combining logic with creativity.

My hobbies include archery, futurist films, traveling, learning new languages and cultures, horse-back riding, music composition in guitar and piano, painting, pottery and eating canned 'Popei' spinach with a spoonful of Crème fraîche.
