Values To Live By

In general, I try to live up to these values:

Disagree 1 ----- 2 ----- 3 ----- 4 ----- 5 Agree

Competence: 4

Compassion: 4

Persistance: 5

Honesty: 5

Wisdom: 5

Persistance is one of the values I try to live by- because I believe that most successes in life comes from persisting in the face of hardship. We face challenges in our lives everyday, whether in our jobs, studies, relationships, or any type of important decisions. Some people may live easy lives, and never need to make any kind of efforts to get what they want. In these cases, it is hard to know who they really are. You can't judge them by what they have, but you're also not able to truly see what aspires and passionates them, because you haven't seen them truly sacrifice or give 100% of themselves to something that they long for.

People who persist tend to show their nature in the purest way. When you persist in doing something, like persisting in succesfully completing a programming bootcamp even though you may not have a technical background or prior knowledge of coding, you reveal how much you are willing to sacrifice and how much effort you are willing to make in order to pursue what truly passionates you. This could mean 13 straight hours of coding everyday, or not seeing any family and friends for a few months until you graduate. In other situations, it could mean giving up your greatest joys in life in order to be more fit and healthy.

Being persistent also shows that you have faith in what you believe. Having faith means that you believe so strongly that your idea is just, or right for you, that you are confident that it is worth doing whatever it takes.
