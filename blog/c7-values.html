Values To Live By

In general, I try to live up to these values:

Disagree 1 ----- 2 ----- 3 ----- 4 ----- 5 Agree

Competence: 4

Compassion: 4

Persistance: 5

Honesty: 5

Wisdom: 5

Persistance is one of the values I try to live by- because I believe that most successes in life comes from persisting in the face of hardship. We face challenges in our lives everyday, whether in our jobs, studies, relationships, or any type of important decisions we make.

Some people may live easy lives, and never need to make any kind of efforts to get what they want. In these cases, it is hard to know who they really are, what aspires them, because you haven't seen them truly sacrifice, or give 100% of themselves, to something that they long for.

In my opinion, people who persist tend to show their nature in the purest way. For example, when you persist in successfully completing an intensive programming bootcamp (despite having no prior knowledge of coding), you reveal you nature by your dedication. Dedication could mean 13 straight hours of coding everyday, or not seeing any family and friends for a few months until you graduate. In other situations, it could mean giving up your greatest joys in life in order to be more fit and healthy.

Being persistent also shows that you have faith in what you believe. You believe so strongly that it is right for you that you are confident that it is worth doing whatever it takes.
